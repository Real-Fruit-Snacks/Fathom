# mesg

> Display or set write permissions for your terminal.
> Other users can write messages to your terminal with `write` or `wall`.
> More information: <https://man7.org/linux/man-pages/man1/mesg.1.html>.

- Display current write permissions for your terminal:

`mesg`

- Disable write permissions for your terminal:

`mesg <n>`

- Enable write permissions for your terminal:

`mesg <y>`
