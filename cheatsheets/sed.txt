# Advanced sed Examples

## Basic substitution
sed 's/old/new/' file.txt

## Global substitution (all occurrences)
sed 's/old/new/g' file.txt

## Case-insensitive substitution
sed 's/old/new/I' file.txt

## In-place editing with backup
sed -i.bak 's/old/new/g' file.txt

## Delete lines matching pattern
sed '/pattern/d' file.txt

## Delete specific line numbers
sed '5d' file.txt

## Delete range of lines
sed '5,10d' file.txt

## Print only matching lines
sed -n '/pattern/p' file.txt

## Multiple operations
sed -e 's/old/new/g' -e '/pattern/d' file.txt

## Insert line before match
sed '/pattern/i\New line before' file.txt

## Append line after match
sed '/pattern/a\New line after' file.txt

## Extract specific fields
sed 's/.*\[\(.*\)\].*/\1/' file.txt

## Replace only on specific lines
sed '5,10s/old/new/g' file.txt

## Use different delimiter
sed 's|/path/to/file|/new/path|g' file.txt

## Remove HTML tags
sed 's/<[^>]*>//g' file.html

## Convert DOS to Unix line endings
sed 's/\r$//' file.txt
